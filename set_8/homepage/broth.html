Broth

Roast, then boil.

  • Pork bones
  • Chicken wings
  • One big potato
  • Salt

Roast the pork bones and chicken wings with some olive oil and salt at 200o C for 30 min. Save some of the fat for the tare, add the rest to the pot. Boil for as long as you have the patience for, at least a day. In the last couple of hours add in the potato. Watch out for the mess and the smell. When done, and the broth has cooled off, take out the bones. Use the immersion blender, then strain. This should result in a thick, rich broth.
